About This Application

Health Checker AI is an innovative web application that uses artificial intelligence to provide preliminary health insights based on your symptoms. Powered by the advanced Jemma-AI model running on Ollama, our platform offers quick, AI-driven health assessments.

Important Disclaimer: This application is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

Key Features

AI-Powered Analysis

Utilizes the Jemma-AI model to analyze symptoms and provide insights

Health History

Track and review your previous health consultations

Secure & Private

Your health data is encrypted and stored securely

Personal Profile

Manage your account and track your health check statistics

Fast Response

Get AI-generated insights within seconds

Responsive Design

Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Database: MySQL (XAMPP)
  • AI Model: Jemma-AI via Ollama
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5
  • Icons: Font Awesome
  • Security: SHA-256 Hashing
